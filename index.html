Welcome to the World of Search Engines

Search engines are programs designed to search for information on the Internet. They index billions of web pages to provide users with the most relevant search results.

Modern search engines process billions of search queries daily

History of search engine development

The history of search engines dates back to the 1990s. Archie, created in 1990, is considered to be the first full-fledged search engine. Since then, search engines have come a long way and have become an integral part of the Internet.

The main types of search engines

  • General search engines - such as Google, Yandex, Bing
  • Meta-search systems - combining the results of several search engines
  • Specialized search engines - for searching for certain types of content
  • Semantic search engines that analyze the meaning of a query
  • Visual search engines - working with images
On each of the pages of our website you will find detailed information about different types of search engines, their principles of operation and usage features.
