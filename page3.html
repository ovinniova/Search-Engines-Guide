Specialized search engines

They focus on certain types of information and resources. Some of them are:

  • People and contact search engines. They often work on the basis of social networks, for example, VKontakte, Facebook, LinkedIn. They can be private and searched through a database of email addresses or phone numbers, for example, employees or customers of the company.
  • Job search engines and resumes. They connect employers and job seekers, for example, HeadHunter, LinkedIn and Glassdoor.
  • Cartographic search engines. Provide information about the locations and maps, as well as provide navigation, for example, "Yandex Maps", 2GIS, Google Maps, Apple Maps.
  • Academic publication search systems.They provide scientific articles, research, and academic literature upon request, for example, Google Scholar and Microsoft Academic.
  • Scientific and technical information search engines.They provide the user with access to specialized data such as patents, technical specifications, etc., for example, "FIPS".
