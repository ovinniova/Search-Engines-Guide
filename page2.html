General search engines

They focus on certain types of information and resources. Some of them are:

  • Yandex. It was founded in 1997 in Russia. In 2000, he launched a search engine with algorithms for the Russian language. It quickly became popular due to its accuracy and convenience. It also develops maps, mail, and cloud services.
  • Google. It was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. I used the PageRank algorithm to evaluate the pages. It has become the world's leading search engine with many services, including Gmail and YouTube.
  • Bing. Launched by Microsoft in 2009, replacing Live Search. He introduced innovations, including visual search and integration with Microsoft services. It is constantly evolving, improving image search and voice search.
  • DuckDuckGo. It was founded in 2008 by Gabriel Weinberg. It is known for protecting privacy — it does not track users and does not collect data. Offers "Instant Answers" for quick answers.
  • Yahoo! It was founded in 1994 as a website catalog. In 1995, it became a search engine. Expanded services, including mail and news. In 2017, it was acquired by Verizon after a decline in popularity.
